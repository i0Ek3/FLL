# Turkish

## Alphabet

![](https://s3-eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/glossika-blog/2021/05/turkish-alphabets.png)

- **Ç** = ch of **ch**air
- **Ğ** = gh of throu**gh**
- **I / ı** = i of s**i**t
- **İ / i** = i of bel**i**eve
- **Ö** = IPA ø or u of n**u**rse
- **Ş** = sh of **sh**ade
- **Ü** = IPA y or French c**u**e
